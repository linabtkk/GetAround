Introduction


Hosts and hoteliers' revenues come from transactions where Getaround, which does not operate its own hotels or vacation rentals, acts as an intermediary between property owners and travelers looking for accommodations.

Getaround takes a commission from each booking, leaving the rest to the host or hotelier.


How Does Getaround Make Money?


Getaround earns money by charging a commission on every booking made through their platform. Getaround is a huge accommodation booking platform, and millions of travelers visit their site to find a hotel or rental for their vacations or stays.

When a booking is made, Getaround charges a commission to the hoteliers or property owners.



Business Model


1. Revenue Model

Getaround’s business model is based on commissions charged on every accommodation booking. This commission can vary depending on different factors, such as the type of property and its popularity.



2. Customer Acquisition

Getaround heavily invests in advertising, whether it’s through search engines, travel platforms, or even through social media and television ads.



3. Value Proposition for the Customer

  • Competitive Pricing: Getaround allows users to compare different accommodations at competitive prices.
  • Wide Selection: The platform offers a wide variety of accommodations, from hotels to vacation homes.
  • Reliability: User reviews and property ratings ensure a secure booking experience.


4. Technological Advantage

Getaround uses cutting-edge technology to make accommodation booking simple and intuitive. Their mobile app offers complete management of bookings, payments, and customer support.
