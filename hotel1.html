Family Suite: The Concierge and The Gallery


Family Suite: The Concierge and The Gallery


five bedrooms , fully equipped kitchen


The Family Suite offers a spacious, comfortable retreat for families. It includes a master bedroom with a king-sized bed and a
separate room with twin or bunk beds for children. The living area features a cozy sofa, flat-screen TV, and a small dining space.


A kitchenette with a mini-fridge and microwave adds convenience for light meals and snacks.
The bathroom includes both a bathtub and walk-in shower, with family-friendly amenities like bath toys and step stools.
Bright and airy, some suites also feature a private balcony, making it the perfect family-friendly getaway.




You have access to :


  • Guided City Tours
  • Cooking Class with a Local Chef
  • Wine Tasting Experience
  • Historical Walking Tours
  • Street Photography Workshop
  • Boat or Kayak Excursion
